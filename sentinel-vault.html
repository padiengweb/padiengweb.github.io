Sentinel-Vault

Real-time mutual authentication to eliminate social engineering scams.

The Problem & Solution

Social engineering accounts for the majority of modern bank fraud. Customers cannot verify if the person calling them is actually a bank employee, and employees struggle to verify customers without relying on easily compromised static data.

Sentinel-Vault solves this by forcing a cryptographic, real-time handshake between both parties before any sensitive account actions can proceed. If the employee cannot authenticate to the customer's app, the call is instantly flagged as a scam.
